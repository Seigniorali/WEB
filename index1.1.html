History

Discography

The discography of the band includes 13 official studio albums, published in 1963-1970, and 211 songs. Each album took the first places in the world charts and lasted for a very long time. Most of The Beatles's compositions are co-authored and signed with the names of John Lennon and Paul McCartney.
